# 10 — The reception redesign (2026-07-14)

The site's third and current design language. Supersedes the slate/harbor theme in
[`07-design-language.md`](07-design-language.md) (which superseded the original
olive/citron Save-the-Date theme). This one is different in kind: it comes from
**Carolyn's own reception design**, not from our interpretation of a vibe.

## Sources (all fetched + analyzed 2026-07-14)

- **Milanote "Wedding Reception" board** (29 images, her actual selections):
  cobalt scallop-edged and rim-striped tableware, cream cross-back chairs +
  bistro flatware, checkerboard dance floor, sailcloth tent, brass pleated-shade
  lamps, poppies-and-citrus florals, linen options (butter moiré, blush ticking
  stripe, garden florals). One note: "Napkin / hightop table linen options:".
- **Her Pinterest boards** via invite links: wedding-decor-inspiration (15 pins),
  florals (15), food-at-wedding (14). Poppies/marigolds/dahlias, oxblood grounding,
  wax-seal stationery, tomato-and-citrus food styling.
- **Four vibe photos from Carolyn** (in `assets-inbox/`, gitignored): flash-film
  ring photo (oxblood nails, red-orange sweater, camel jacket, gold rings), antique
  chintz wallpaper (cream + orange blooms + sage birds), a sage landscape toile with
  literally scallop-patterned fields, and a vintage sea-fairy illustration (dusty
  blue wave, blush fish). They validated the direction rather than changing it.
- Reference archive (boards, pins, contact sheets) lives in the session scratchpad;
  re-fetch technique documented in memory (headless Chromium renders both Milanote
  share links and Pinterest invite boards; pin JSON is embedded in the board HTML).

## How it was decided

A 10-agent workflow: 5 visual analysts (one per image batch) + 1 current-site token
reader → 1 synthesis → 3 adversarial verifiers (WCAG contrast math on 13 pairs — all
pass AA; a fidelity skeptic re-viewing the source images; a web-craft skeptic).
Both skeptics returned "needs-adjustment" with fixes, all adopted:

- Keep `--brick` **oxblood** (her boards ground the orange in wine tones); bright
  poppy became its own `--poppy` token instead of overwriting it.
- Add `--blush` (a co-lead accent across the boards that synthesis missed).
- Merge the proposed two cobalts (1.16:1 apart — imperceptible) into one.
- Blue stays **graphic edge, not field**: her boards have no large solid blue; cap
  one cobalt band per page (the natural state of every current page) + footer.
- Photo overlays derive from desaturated navy-indigo `#202A55`, not raw cobalt —
  full chroma casts purple on the coastal photos. Verified by screenshot.
- Hero names stay cream over photos (poppy script at 3:1 is illegible); bright
  poppy type is allowed only at display sizes on cream.
- Links scoped per surface: burnt poppy on cream, cream-underlined on cobalt
  (burnt poppy on cobalt is 1.9:1 — never).
- Plate-rim button needs three inset shadow stops (fill-colored gap), not two.
- No hover-only state signals: the nav's active dot is `aria-current`-driven;
  hover glows are decorative desktop garnish.

Type (revised same day, Patrick's one-voice rule): **Mulish is the default for ALL
text** — body, leads, labels, nav tabs, buttons, dates, calendars. **Cormorant
appears only on h1/h2/h3** as the display face (600 on cobalt/photo grounds). The
couple's names are never set in type: they're the hand-lettered wordmark image.
Sacramento is gone from the font import. Don't reintroduce serif body/UI text.

## The three signatures (used consistently, nothing else)

1. **Plate-edge scallop seam** (`assets/svg/scallop-seam.svg`): cream half-discs
   with a cobalt rim line at every cream→cobalt section seam and above the footer.
   (It also rhymes with the fish-scale fields in her toile and the sea-fairy wave.)
2. **Plate-rim double ring** on `.btn--ink` (triple inset shadow) and a double rim
   rule under centered section h2s; hover/active/focus = burnt poppy fill.
3. **Poppy line art** (`assets/svg/poppy-divider.svg`): monoline poppy on a looping
   stem in the monogram's hand — poppy cup, oxblood bud + stamens, stem-green line,
   blush fallen petal. Replaces the coupes divider (coupes retired to the footer).

Garnish: wax-seal copper dots on the schedule timeline, clementine `aria-current`
dot in the nav, marigold candlelit card-hover glow, blush gallery-hover glow.
